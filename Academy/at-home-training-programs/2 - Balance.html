At home training program for lesson 3 - Balance

The following are some conditioning exercises to accompany the in person classes. They are designed as strengths and conditioning exersises specific for parkour athletes.

It is recommended that they are split into two separate days, but it is up to the individual athlete to perform them in the order and amount they want.

Day 1:


Full Body Circuit Strength and Conditioning

In this video the exercises first explained and demonstrated and then performed Watch the explanation and then follow along the demonstration. You can do more than 1 round. 1 round is the bare minimum, 2 rounds are good, and 3 rounds are a complete workout.

Contents:

Exercises with timestamps

  1. Jump/Landin (0:22).
  2. Step throughs (0:58).
  3. Ab and core exercise (1:42).
  4. Skater squat (2:03).
  5. 360 push-up (2:58).
  6. Full list with reps(4:39).

Regimen recomentation:

One round contains the following:
  1. 6 Jump/Landins
  2. 20 Step-throughs
  3. 12 Crunch/Reverse crunch
  4. 6 Skater squats left
  5. 6 Skater squats right
  6. 12 360 push-up

Day 2:


The videos below form a sellection of parkour focused exercises. Perform them at least once in the order you prefer. Warm up the joints you are going to use before each exercise.


Balance Reach Game

Do only the last exersise (3:24). Spent only 2-5 minutes.

Balance Challenge

Do exercise 2 (1:53).

Rail Balance

Do 21 (or more) repetitions. Performing it with correct technique is more important than getting a lot of height.

Sideways wall squat

Do a 21 squats (or more) for each leg.

Rail walk foot tap (back & forth)

Do a 30 taps (or more) for each leg.

Wall edge walk (back & forth)

Walk at the edge of a sidewalk. Step on the edge with the same footing as if it's a rail. Do an even number of teps befor changing direction in order to work both legs equally.

Forward rail squat (feet apart)

Use a presission trainer or sidewalk edge. Don't start on a high railing! Di 1 set of 31.

180째 wall pivots

In the videos bellow 180째 wall pivots both from high and low possition are demonstrated. Do 2 sets of 30. Start from a high position move to low position if during the 2nd set it start to feel very easy.

90째 wall hops

Do a 30 wall hops (or more). Meake sure you do equal numbers on each side. You can use stairs, a sidewalk or domething with an edge. Be carefull not to fall and be aware of your surroundings.

180째 wall hops

Same as the above. Only do this if you where succesfull with the previous exersise and it can be done safely.
