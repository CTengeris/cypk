At home training program for lesson 4 - Climb-ups & Swings

The following are some conditioning exercises to accompany the in person classes. They are designed as strengths and conditioning exersises specific for parkour athletes.

It is recommended that they are split into two separate days, but it is up to the individual athlete to perform them in the order and amount they want.

Day 1:


Full Body High Intensity Circuit for Parkour

In this video the exercises first explained and demonstrated and then performed Watch the explanation and then follow along the demonstration. Do more than 1 round or more. 1 complete workout.

Contents:

Exercises with timestamps

  1. Mountain climbers (0:48).
  2. Twisted mountain climbers (1:05).
  3. Step-though (1:21).
  4. Ground kong-ups (1:36).
  5. Flared mountain climbers (1:59).
  6. Switching step-throughs (2:13).
  7. Inverted mountain climbers (2:35).

Regimen recomentation:

One round contains the following:
  1. 60 mountain climbers
  2. 30 twisting mountaqin climbers
  3. 20 step-thoughs
  4. 10 gound kong-ups
  1. 60 mountain climbers
  2. 30 flared mountain climbers
  3. 20 switching step-thoughs
  4. 10 inverted mountain climders

Day 2:


The videos below form a sellection of parkour focused exercises. Perform them at least once in the order you prefer. Warm up the joints you are going to use before each exercise.


Bar hangs

Hang from a bar trying to stay on as long as possible. Try and see waht your personal record is. Try both grip styles with breaks in between attempts.

Alternativly a progression to the above exersise is to do the same with tome limited assistans from the legs. If the above is to hard try this in its place.

Wall dip

Do 4 sets of 5-9 repetitions.

Alternativly you can do an easier progression.

Half climb-down

Do 21 repetitions.

Cat hang high pull with foothold

Do 4 sets of 5-9 repetitions.

360 support shimmy (pool-assist)

Step 1 get a house with a pool. Step 2 follow along the video bellow.
