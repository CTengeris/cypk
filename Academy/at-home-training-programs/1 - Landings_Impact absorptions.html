At home training program for lesson 1 - Landings/Impact absorptions

The following are some conditioning exercises to accompany the in person classes. They are designed as strengths and conditioning exersises specific for parkour athletes.

It is recommended that they are split into two separate days, but it is up to the individual athlete to perform them in the order and amount they want.

Day 1:


Full Body Circuit Strength and Conditioning

In this video the exercises first explained and demonstrated and then performed Watch the explanation and then follow along the demonstration. You can do more than 1 round. 1 round is the bare minimum, 2 rounds are good, and 3 rounds are a complete workout.

Contents:

Exercises with timestamps

  1. Jump/Landin (0:22).
  2. Step throughs (0:58).
  3. Ab and core exercise (1:42).
  4. Skater squat (2:03).
  5. 360 push-up (2:58).
  6. Full list with reps(4:39).

Regimen recomentation:

One round contains the following:
  1. 6 Jump/Landins
  2. 20 Step-throughs
  3. 12 Crunch/Reverse crunch
  4. 6 Skater squats left
  5. 6 Skater squats right
  6. 12 360 push-up

Day 2:


The videos below form a sellection of parkour focused exercises. Perform them at least once in the order you prefer. Warm up the joints you are going to use before each exercise.


Balance and Precision Challenges

From the video below you can do exersise 1 (0:46) at any point during the day. Do exercise 2 (1:53) together with the rest of the exercise in this program.

Balance Reach Game

Follow along the instuctor in the video bellow.

Vertical jump/landings

Do 11 (minimum) of each of the types in the following videos. Performing it with correct technique is more important than getting a lot of height.

Broad jumps

Do a 21 broad jumps (or more). Use any style from the following videos (the first and second type are the most useful give emphasis on them). Distance is not very important, have long enough distance to feel like a jump but short enough to be easy so you can have a correct execution.
